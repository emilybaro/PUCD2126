    Stalls: 1 2 3 4 5

    White Star’s offers a variety of stall sizes depending on your horse’s size and needs. There is a barn that is structured for the proportions of a large horse as well as a barn for small horses and ponies.

    Pastures: 1 2 3 4 5

    The property features 5 medium sized pastures that stand side by side. There are few rocks and the quality of the grass is overall good, although horses are not turned out on weekends in order to allow the grass to grow. Otherwise, horses are turned out every day and are brushed upon entering the barn after turnout.

    Staff: 1 2 3 4 5

    The staff includes three grooms, a barn manager, a trainer, and an onsite employee. People who work at white star genuinely care for horses, and you can tell that it isn’t just a job for them. Horses have round the clock care, seven days a week.

    Hay/Feed: 1 2 3 4 5

    Each horse at White Star has a custom meal plan depending on it’s needs, some horses are fed once a day and others are fed lunch and snacks, whatever works best for them. Horses are only fed the highest quality feed and all is included in the full board price of $550.

    Maintenance: 1 2 3 4 5

    The facility is kept clean and neat at all times. Attention is given daily to both the barn, onsite trails, arena and round pen, as well as the riders lounge.

    Tackroom: 1 2 3 4 5

    This airconditioned tackroom includes brushes, flyspray, replaceable parts for equipment and a medicine cabinet for both horse and rider. Tack is kept clean and organized, although the saddle racks could be of better quality.

    Ambience: 1 2 3 4 5

    White Star’s ambience is unmatched. The breathtaking scenery along with the relaxed, friendly atmosphere makes anyone feel like they are in a fairy land far away. Anybody who steps onto the property tends to forget about all their worries and just enjoy their surroundings, whether they enjoy horses or not.

    Lounge: 1 2 3 4 5

    The lounge is clean, comfortable, and airconditioned. It features a two restrooms, a couch, television, dining table and chairs, microwave, bathroom, and even a laundry machine incase riders and boarders have any tack that needs to be washed.

    Arenas: 1 2 3 4 5

    There are two arenas on property. The larger arena is the size of a standard show arena and is grounded with grass as well as a sand border. There are trail obstacles and jumps for riders use. The second arena is the round pen which is covered, lit, and air conditioned.

    Restrooms: 1 2 3 4 5

    There are two restrooms located in the riders lounge which are spacious, modern, and cleaned daily.
