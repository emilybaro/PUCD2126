    • La Hacienda Isabella offers a large, sturdy border barn as well as large pastures.

    Stalls: 1 2 3 4 5

    Hacienda La Isabella offers large stalls for horses of any shape or size. The stalls are for the most part kept clean, although there are periods where they 'slack' on their shaving delivery, sometimes obligating the horses to stand on harder surfaces than they should.

    Pastures: 1 2 3 4 5

    The property is mostly made up of pastures which could fit at least five horses each. Unfortunately the turnout is up to the owners doing as it is not often done by the groom or anyone working at the facility. FYI, the owner of the property does specify that daily turnout is a part of full board.

    Staff: 1 2 3 4 5

    The staff is made up of the owner of the barn, who checks in multiple times a week, but does not work on the property himself. There is a groom that lives in a trailer on property. He is not very friendly, but he works hard and keeps the facility very clean.

    Hay/Feed: 1 2 3 4 5

    Each horse is fed one scoop of grain in the morning and in the evening as well as hay cubes. Any other grain or hay preferences must be purchased and most likely administered by the boarder themselves. The feed provided is the cheapest offered, at $10/bag. Not the best quality food for the cost of the full board.

    Maintenance: 1 2 3 4 5

    The facility is kept relatively clean, although itâ€™s cleanliness is at the boarders control. There is very seldom any additional cleaning done by the barn staff.

    Tackroom: 1 2 3 4 5

    There are multiple tackrooms for borders and trainers. They are very small, the size of a closet, and their cleanliness is up to the owner as they are the only ones permitted to enter.

    Ambience: 1 2 3 4 5

    The overall ambience of this barn is good. The owner occassionally hosts a huge party of over 300 people unnanounced which makes it a bit difficult to ride. But when there are no parties, the property is one of the more relaxing and peaceful to ride at.

    Lounge: 1 2 3 4 5

    There is no lounge at this barn. Only a cover by the arena including picnic tables and benches.

    Arenas: 1 2 3 4 5

    There are two arenas on property. Both are grass, although one has a sounding board and sand path. The other is a large grass field that includes two circle paths for riding and training. There are no rocks in the arenas and they are kept relatively clean.

    Restrooms: 1 2 3 4 5

    There is both a mens and womans restroom on property, they are large and have potential, but are unfortunately not well kept in the slightest bit.
