    • MIRC offers various sand arenas with state of the art jumps in each.

    Stalls: 1 2 3 4 5

    Miami Internation Riding Club’s stalls are large, modern, state of the art stalls which are both beautiful and modern including automatic water feeders, rubber padding, clean shavings, and an individual fan for each horse.

    Pastures: 1 2 3 4 5

    This property features numerous large pastures. Provides individual day or night turnout depending on what works best for borders and horses. If horses are turned out at night then they are fed in their pastures. There is, however, very little grass in most pastures and for some there are too many rocks.

    Staff: 1 2 3 4 5

    This large training and boarding facility is family owned and run. The owners of the barn are a mother and daughter and have also hired two additional coaches for their lesson program. Apart from trainers, there are various grooms, some of which live onsite.

    Hay/Feed: 1 2 3 4 5

    Horses at Miami International Riding Club are two to three times a day depending on your horses needs. The barn uses Nutrena Elite feeding products and gives horses hay two to three times a day depending on your horses needs.

    Maintenance: 1 2 3 4 5

    The facility is kept clean and organized by its many grooms and staff. Stalls are cleaned one or two times daily depending on board choice, standard or deluxe.

    Tackroom: 1 2 3 4 5

    This barn features three fully equipped tackrooms. One is exclusively for boarders, another is for lessons only, and one mutual tackroom for both. All tackrooms are new, clean, and organized.

    Ambience: 1 2 3 4 5

    Although this is a stricter training faclility, the barn is kept fun and lively, offering it’s students monthly show experiences as well as field trips and family oriented activities. The facility includes various spots for riders to relax and enjoy the space, such as their koi pond at the front of the facility.

    Lounge: 1 2 3 4 5

    Although there is no designated lounge, the air conditioned tackrooms do have couches for students and boarders to sit in and cool off from a ride. Apart from that, there are tiki huts adjacent to the arenas that allow you to sit in the shade and observe lessons.

    Arenas: 1 2 3 4 5

    The property features two arenas and two round pens. The arena’s have dimensions of 230 x 175 feet and 100 x 200 feet. Both have numerous different kinds of jumps for riders use.

    Restrooms: 1 2 3 4 5

    This barn features airconditioned restrooms and showers which are airconditioned and kept clean for rider and boarder use.
