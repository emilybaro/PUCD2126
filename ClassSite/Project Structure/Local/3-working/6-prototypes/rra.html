    Stalls: 1 2 3 4 5

    The stalls at the Redland Riding Academy are suitable for horses of all sizes, although horses over 16hh may feel a bit snug. The stalls are equipped with sufficient shavings and are cleaned daily.

    Pastures: 1 2 3 4 5

    This property offers over five large pastures. Each pasture is slightly different, some having more trees, or more grass, or more soil. All have some kind of shade for horses to escape the sun under. Horses are turned out in groups, there is no individual turnout.

    Staff: 1 2 3 4 5

    The RRA is family owned, operated, and lives on-site. Other than Susie Petersen, there are a few coaches who offer lessons and help with trail rides. There is a groom on-site who feeds and cleans stalls, although Susie does a lot of the work herself.

    Hay/Feed: 1 2 3 4 5

    Horses at the Redland Riding Academy are all for the most part fed the same amount of feed, this being a scoop of grain and a pad of hay in the morning and evening.

    Maintenance: 1 2 3 4 5

    This barn is clean and well kept. It does have a bit of a "western dude ranch" feel to it and isn't kept as pristine as MIRC, but in my opinion, as long as it doesn't har, the horses or riders, it's all part of the fun.

    Tackroom: 1 2 3 4 5

    The tackroom is kept relatively clean. There is a lesson tackroom with the lesson tack as well as a separate tackroom for borders. The tackrooms are not airconditioned but keep the equipment safe.

    Ambience: 1 2 3 4 5 <

    This facility has a warm, wonderful, casual ambience for all ages. Whether you are riding or just hanging out, there is a shady space for everyone. Susie and coaches do a great job keeping their lesson program fun and interesting with events such as camping trail rides, road trips, and easter egg hunts.

    Lounge: 1 2 3 4 5

    Other than the room leading into the bathroom that is joint with a border tackroom, there is no official air conditioned lounge at this barn. As mentioned earlier, there are multiple cool, outdoor covered areas for relaxing by the arena and in the cowboy saloon.

    Arenas: 1 2 3 4 5

    There is one arena on property. It is quite small, but the sand is of good quality and the space is safe.

    Restrooms: 1 2 3 4 5

    There is one restroom on property. It is large, clean, and well kept.
