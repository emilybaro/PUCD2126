    Stalls: 1 2 3 4 5

    Imperial Farms features large stalls for all horse sizes, ranging from pony to Clydesdale. The stalls are cleaned multiple times a day and are always cushioned with both rubber mats as well as a suffiecient amount of good quality shavings.

    Pastures: 1 2 3 4 5

    There are many large, safe pastures on property. Horses can be turned out in groups or individually depending on what works best for them. Both day and night turnout are offered at the boarders preference.

    Staff: 1 2 3 4 5

    The owners of this barn live on-site and are very involved in the well being of the horses and the facilities. There is one groom on property who cleans and feeds.

    Hay/Feed: 1 2 3 4 5

    Horses here are all fed based on their needs. Larger horses recieve more feed and smaller horses recieve less. Both grain and hay is given multiple times a day.

    Maintenance: 1 2 3 4 5

    The facility is kept very clean and organized.

    Tackroom: 1 2 3 4 5

    There is large tackroom for boarders at this barn. It is kept relatively clean and keeps the tack protected. It is not air conditioned but there is a large refrigerator for keeping any food or drinks cold.

    Ambience: 1 2 3 4 5

    This barn has a very fun, laid back atmosphere. There is a variety of disciplines who ride here so everyone is usually open to som efun and trying something new.

    Lounge: 1 2 3 4 5

    There is no designated lounge on property but there are various covered outdoor areas for borders to relax which include benches, chairs, sofa's, and hammocks next to the arena.

    Arenas: 1 2 3 4 5

    There is one grass dressage arena on property. It is well kept and unlike any barn in the South Florida area, this arena is covered, and can completely be zipped shut for riding in any weather.

    Lounge: 1 2 3 4 5

    There is one restroom on the property. It is small but kept relatively clean.
